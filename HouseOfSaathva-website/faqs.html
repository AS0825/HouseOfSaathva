Frequently Asked Questions

House of Saathva is based in Mangalore, Karnataka, India. We cover weddings across Mangalore, Udupi, Bangalore, Goa, and are available for destination weddings anywhere in India and worldwide.

Yes, absolutely. We are available worldwide. Travel and accommodation costs for out of Mangalore weddings are charged separately and will be communicated transparently before booking.

Our crew varies by package and event. A typical full wedding coverage includes a lead cinematographer, a traditional videographer, a candid photographer, and a traditional photographer. We customise the crew based on your specific celebration.

Deliverables depend on your package but typically include a cinematic wedding highlight film, Instagram reels for each event, a curated set of fully edited photographs, and all raw files. We handover everything on a hard drive and via cloud link.

Edited highlights and reels are delivered within 30-45 days. Full-length films and complete photo albums may take up to 60-75 days. We will always give you a clear timeline at the time of booking.

We follow a three part payment: 30% advance at the time of booking, 40% on the wedding day, and the remaining 30% after final delivery and corrections. Full package pricing will be shared during our initial conversation.
