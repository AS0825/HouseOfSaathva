The Name Behind the Work

The Name Behind the Work

The word Saathva is inspired by the idea of Saath togetherness, companionship, and belonging.

A wedding is not just the union of two individuals. It is the coming together of families, traditions, memories, and generations.

The word House represents a creative house dedicated to preserving stories, emotions, and legacies.

Together, House of Saathva stands for a place where love, family, and togetherness are celebrated and preserved forever.

— Jithesh Devadiga
Our Philosophy

We Believe Weddings Are Not Events. They Are Stories.

Stories of love. Stories of family. Stories of belonging. Stories of legacy.

Every wedding carries emotions that often go unnoticed the glance, the pause, the touch, the silence, the tears, the laughter. These are the moments we seek to preserve.

Because years from now, people may forget what happened.

But they will always remember how it felt.
